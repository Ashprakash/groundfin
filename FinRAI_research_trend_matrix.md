## Decision Signal

The highest-opportunity cluster is financial agents plus evidence/hallucination/spreadsheet reliability. Older papers such as BloombergGPT, FinGPT, PIXIU, FinanceBench, and FinBen provide the citation anchors. Newer 2025-2026 papers show where the field is moving, but most have low current citation counts because they are recent. This creates a timing window for a FinRAI extension that defines a responsible evaluation layer for agentic financial decision systems.

Recommended paper direction:

**FinRAI-AgentBench: A Responsible Evaluation Contract and Evidence Bundle for Financial LLM Agents**

Core claim:

Existing benchmarks evaluate financial QA, tool use, retrieval, spreadsheets, or professional knowledge, but they do not provide a unified responsible-evaluation contract for financial decision agents that jointly measures evidence support, hallucination, calibration, refusal/abstention, timeliness, compliance risk, reproducibility, and cost.
